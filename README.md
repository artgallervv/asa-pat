## ASA PAT
*Give headpats to Asa Mitaka from Chainsaw Man.*
> I wanted to make this minigame because I think Asa Mitaka is very cool, and she deserves lots of hugs and love.
### Features
- Give headpats to Asa Mitaka on both computer and mobile.
- She will get mad if you stop patting her head, and she will be happy if you pat her for enough time.
- That's about it I think,,, it's a pretty simple game.
